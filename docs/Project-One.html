Ngee Ann Digital Legacy Trail
How might we create an interactive Memory Trail that brings Ngee Ann Polytechnic’s history to life and fosters pride and sense of belonging within the community?

In this project, my team and I worked on a real-world initiative aimed at integrating technology into the polytechnic’s library to creatively showcase and promote the school’s heritage. We conducted site visits, user research, and stakeholder interviews to gain a deeper understanding of user needs.

Based on our findings, we proposed practical solutions and developed prototypes, including an interactive game in Unity Engine and a mobile app in Figma, to highlighted key aspects of the school’s history in an engaging and accessible way
